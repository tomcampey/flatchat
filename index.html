How the Buckets Work

Flatchat uses a spaced repetition system with 5 buckets. Cards move up when you answer correctly in both directions (your language and target language), and drop back to New if either direction is wrong.

  • New Review immediately — cards you haven't learned yet
  • Learning Review after 1 day — just getting started
  • Familiar Review after 3 days — you're getting the hang of it
  • Known Review after 7 days — solid knowledge
  • Mastered Review after 14 days — well learned!

Language Benchmarks

How many words do you need to know to use a language?

  • 500 words — enough to navigate tourist situations and basic introductions
  • 1,000–3,000 words — conversational level, handling everyday situations like shopping, asking how people are, discussing your day
  • 3,000 words — 95% coverage of everyday texts and conversations
  • 5,000 words — about 98% coverage, allowing accurate learning from context
